**About the speaker:** Sigrid Passano Hellan is a machine learning researcher with a strong interest in climate applications. She is currently a senior researcher at NORCE Norwegian Research Centre and is affiliated with the Bjerknes Centre for Climate Research. Sigrid recently completed her PhD in Data Science from the School of Informatics at the University of Edinburgh. Her research interests encompass Bayesian optimisation, the construction of priors, and machine learning applications within the climate and environmental domains. Her doctoral research, supervised by Nigel Goddard and Christopher Lucas, involved applying Bayesian optimisation to air pollution monitoring.
